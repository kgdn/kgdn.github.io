Hi, I'm Kieran. I'm a third year Computer Systems student at Heriot-Watt University, Edinburgh, Scotland. I made this blog as a way to share my thoughts and experiences with others. I hope you enjoy it!

## What will be on this site?
I'm currently working on this site, but I'm not sure what I'll be putting here yet. Generally speaking, I'll probably talk about random things, including GNU/Linux, programming, my hobbies, my thoughts and feelings about life, the world and politics. You know... the usual stuff teenagers talk about.

## What was this website made in?
The website was written using [Markdown](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Markdown) and [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/). This website is being hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). To actually make the website, I used Visual Studio Code with a few extensions.
